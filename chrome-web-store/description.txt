Reduce distracting LinkedIn surfaces while keeping LinkedIn useful. LinkedIn
Feed Blocker lets you block the Home feed, right-rail distractions, and selected
My Network recommendation modules so you can use LinkedIn more intentionally.

Features:

- Blocks the LinkedIn Home feed
- Blocks Home right-rail widgets and ad-like distractions
- Blocks My Network puzzle, Premium upsell, and suggestions sections
- Keeps higher-value LinkedIn areas such as jobs, messages, search, profiles,
  direct links, and My Network invitations available
- Turn blocking on or off for each supported section separately
- Use a compact popup or keyboard shortcut to toggle supported pages
- Saves settings in your browser with Chrome extension storage

Use LinkedIn for the parts you came for without leaving every recommendation
loop open by default.
